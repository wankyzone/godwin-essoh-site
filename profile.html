Academic Profile

Godwin Edet Essoh is a senior academic and public administration scholar with extensive experience in teaching, research, academic leadership, and institutional governance. He currently serves as a Senior Lecturer and Acting Dean of the Faculty of Social and Management Sciences at Ritman University, Ikot Ekpene, Nigeria.

He holds a Doctor of Philosophy (Ph.D.) in Public Administration and Policy Analysis, a Master of Public Administration (MPA), and a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Management. His academic training spans Nigeria and the United States, providing a broad comparative perspective on governance and institutional development.

Research Interests

  • Public Administration and Public Policy
  • Employment Relations and Human Resource Management
  • Governance and Institutional Reform
  • Peace, Conflict Resolution, and Development Studies
  • Public Sector Performance and Workforce Development

Professional Memberships

  • Fellow, Chartered Institute of Administration (FCIA)
  • Fellow, Institute of Corporate Administration
