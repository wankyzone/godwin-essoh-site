Academic Leadership & Administration

Dr. Godwin Edet Essoh has held several senior academic leadership and administrative positions across Nigerian universities. His leadership experience spans faculty administration, departmental management, academic governance, and programme accreditation.

Current Appointments

  • Acting Dean, Faculty of Social and Management Sciences, Ritman University (2022 – Present)
  • Chairman, Senate Committee on Mid-Semester Examinations, Ritman University (2025 – Present)
  • Coordinator, Department of Political Science & Public Administration, Ritman University (2025 – Present)

Previous Leadership Roles

  • Head of Department, Employment Relations & Human Resource Management, Ritman University (2015 – 2022)
  • Head of Department, Management, Ritman University (2020 – 2022)
  • Director, Centre for Entrepreneurship, Ritman University (2016 – 2023)
  • Programme Coordinator, Peace and Development Studies, Salem University (2013 – 2015)

University Governance & Committees

  • Member, University Senate, Ritman University
  • Member, Senate Committee on Staff Discipline
  • Member, Examinations Committee
  • Member, Academic Planning Committee
  • Member, Senate Committee on Campus Discipline
  • Member, Senate Committee on NUC BMAS Quality Assurance
  • Chairman, Senate Committee on Carry-Over Programme

Programme Accreditation & Institutional Impact

Dr. Essoh has successfully led multiple academic programmes through National Universities Commission (NUC) accreditation exercises, achieving full accreditation status across departments and faculties under his leadership.
