Senior Lecturer, Public Administration & Human Resource Management

I am a senior academic and public administration scholar with extensive experience in teaching, research, academic leadership, and institutional governance. Academic Focus

My academic interests span Public Administration, Employment Relations, Human Resource Management, and Development Studies. I engage in research that addresses institutional reform, conflict resolution, workforce performance, and socio-economic development in Africa and other developing economies.

Academic Leadership & Service

I have served in several leadership and governance roles including Acting Dean, Head of Department, Chairman of Senate Committees, and Director of Entrepreneurship Development. I have led academic programmes through successful National Universities Commission (NUC) accreditation exercises and contributed to institutional planning, quality assurance, and academic policy formulation.
